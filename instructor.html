About instructor

Julianne Sterling is a Principal Product Designer with over 10 years of experience creating enterprise design systems, SaaS platforms, and user-centered digital experiences. She has mentored thousands of designers worldwide... and specializes in Design Systems, Accessibility, UX Research, and Product Design. Her work has shaped the visual language of companies across fintech, edtech, and healthcare — and her teaching philosophy centers on learning by building real, production-quality work.
