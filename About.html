Our Journey So Far

From a small startup in a garage to a global educational leader. Here's how we evolved.

2018

The Seed is Planted

Founded in San Francisco by three university professors who saw the need for a more dynamic, skill-based approach to online learning.

2020

Pivoting Global

In response to the global shift in education, we scaled our infrastructure to support 100k+ concurrent users.

2021

Series A

Raised $25M in funding to integrate AI-driven personalized learning paths for every student.

2023

Learnova 2.0

Launched our mobile ecosystem and vocational certification programs worldwide.
